Setting your RAM.
Definition of the RAM allocated to your game, a larger quantity will increase the performance of your game.
Please note that you should avoid putting too large a quantity otherwise it can cause performance losses on your other applications.

- Maximum RAM: Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It is recommended not to exceed 50% of the total amount of RAM present on your PC so that it can function properly. If your computer has more than 8GB of RAM, we recommend allocating 3GB. If your computer has 4GB or less, do not allocate more than 2GB.

- Minimum RAM: Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated when starting your game. It is recommended not to put more than 3GB for this value. The typical value is 1GB.
